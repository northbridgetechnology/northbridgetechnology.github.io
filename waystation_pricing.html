Common Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

What exactly does the license cover?

Your annual license gives you the right to run Waystation on your infrastructure for the license term, plus access to all software updates and new features released during that period.

Is support required?

No. Support is completely optional. If you have an in-house IT team comfortable managing the stack, you can run Waystation license-only and add support later if needed.

What happens when my license expires?

Your instance keeps running — we don't phone home or enforce kill switches. You simply won't receive further updates until you renew. Your data is always yours.

Are there really no per-seat fees?

None. Hire 10 agents or 100 — your license price doesn't change within your tier. The only time pricing changes is if your team grows beyond your current tier's agent limit.

Do you handle deployment?

Enterprise support includes white-glove deployment assistance. Business and Starter tiers include guided documentation. Waystation ships as a single Docker Compose file — most teams are live within an hour.

Can we try before we buy?

Absolutely. Request a demo and we'll walk you through a live instance. We can also discuss a short evaluation period for Enterprise prospects — get in touch.
